Star Wars Dashboard

Welcome to the Star Wars Dashboard This provides an engaging overview of the galaxy's most crowded planets and popular characters. The Planets Analysis section ranks planets by population, with Coruscant leading at 1 trillion residents, followed by major hubs like Skako and Geonosis. In the Character Analysis, we explore gender distribution, where 40% of characters are human, and screen time rankings reveal that R2-D2 is the most prominent character with 420 minutes, alongside other favorites like C-3PO and Luke Skywalker. Conversely, lesser-known figures like Queen Jamillia and Cliegg Lars occupy the least screen time. Additionally, the dashboard highlights species diversity through a visual comparison of heights, providing a comprehensive and data-driven look into the richly detailed Star Wars universe.
